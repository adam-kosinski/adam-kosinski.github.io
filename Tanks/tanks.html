Your goal: shoot the other tank, without being shot yourself.

Bullets bounce off walls. You have a max of 10 bullets that can be bouncing around at any given time. If you use them all, you have to wait for some to despawn before you can shoot again.

Red Tank Controls (use right hand for EDSF, left hand for Q):

  • E to move forwards
  • D to move backwards
  • S to rotate counterclockwise
  • F to rotate clockwise
  • Q to shoot

Green Tank Controls (use right hand for arrow keys, left hand for M):

  • ↑ to move forwards
  • ↓ to move backwards
  • ← to rotate counterclockwise
  • → to rotate clockwise
  • M to shoot
