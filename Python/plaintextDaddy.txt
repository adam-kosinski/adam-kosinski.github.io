Recently, while searching through a dictionary, Angela beheld her sister bawling loudly and crying keenly. A napkin helped with her streaming bright tears. Angela immediately forged ahead through countless pages within the enormous stiff dictionary. Finally, she found the wanted important insight that let time pass less painfully. Busy Angela needed advice about reading very messy essays, but even she, the best student, trying so deliberately in unraveling all the ambiguous verbs, needed to look sometimes in the large, nifty hardcover reference - her useful dictionary.
